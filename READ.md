1. Introduction

Mental health is an important aspect of overall well-being, but many people do not regularly monitor their emotional state. MoodNova is a web-based application designed to help users track their mood and stress levels in a simple and effective way. It provides insights and recommendations based on user responses, helping individuals manage stress and improve mental health.

2. Problem Statement

Many individuals experience stress, anxiety, and mood fluctuations but lack tools to monitor and understand their emotional patterns. There is a need for a simple system that can:

Track mood regularly
Analyze stress levels
Provide helpful suggestions
3. Objective
To develop a user-friendly mood tracking system
To assess stress levels using a questionnaire
To provide personalized recommendations
To store and visualize user mood history
4. System Description

MoodNova allows users to create an account and log in securely. After logging in, users can take a mood and stress assessment consisting of multiple questions. Based on the answers, the system calculates a mood score and categorizes the stress level.

The system provides suggestions such as relaxation techniques, exercise, or mindfulness practices. It also stores past data and displays it in the form of graphs and history records for better understanding.

8. Advantages
Easy to use interface
Helps in stress management
Tracks emotional patterns

9. Limitations
Not a substitute for professional medical advice

10. Conclusion

MoodNova is an effective application that helps users monitor their mental health in a simple and interactive way. It encourages awareness and promotes healthy habits by providing useful insights and recommendations.
